Previously Asked FAQ's?

A homestay is a type of accommodation where guests stay in the home of a local host, often experiencing a more authentic and local way of life. Homestays can include private rooms, shared rooms, or entire homes, and may also include meals or other services. Homestays differ from hotels in that they offer a more personal and immersive experience, often with a greater focus on cultural exchange and community.

To find a homestay on our website, you can browse through listings by location, price, or other criteria. Once you've found a homestay that you're interested in, you can book directly through the website by providing your contact information and payment details.

Our website checks and verifies the authenticity of all listings and reviews before publishing them on the website. The host's profile, reviews, and ratings are also available for the guests to see.

The cancellation policy for a homestay booking may vary depending on the specific listing and host. You can review the cancellation policy for a specific homestay on the listing page before booking.

You can communicate with the host through our messaging platform on the website. Before your stay, you can ask questions about the homestay, the local area, or the host's own interests and recommendations. During your stay, you can ask the host for help or advice, or simply let them know if everything is going well.

It depends on the host's policy, please check the listing or ask the host before booking.

The minimum and maximum stay may vary depending on the specific homestay and the host's preferences. You can review this information on the listing page before booking.

You can make a payment through our website using a credit card or other accepted payment methods.

The host will provide you with the address and key (or any other necessary access information) for the homestay either through the website's messaging platform or in person upon arrival.

The check-in and check-out time may vary depending on the specific homestay and the host's preferences. You can review this information on the listing page before booking.
